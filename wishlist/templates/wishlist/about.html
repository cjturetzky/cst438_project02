How to use our website


First thing first you need to create an account and login.

Our Website is a place where you are able to store a wishlist of various items that you retrieve from the the internet. Why write down your list on a piece of paper that you can lose when you can keep all the things you want in one place without the fear of being able to lose the information.



Creating a Wishlist

When you create an account you will be granted access to a wishlist that you can add different items from other websites.



Adding a Wish

When you create a wish, you need to make sure you have:

  • a photo (optional)
  • a name
  • a description
  • a url to the product

Then you put the appropriate pieces in their respective places and submit the wish to add it to your total wishlist.



Editing a Wish

Do not worry if you make a mistake when creating your wish as you will be able to go back and edit the items information.
